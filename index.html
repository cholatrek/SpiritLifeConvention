Sprit Life Convention


Activating the Law of the Spirit of life.

Spirit Life convention is an annual convocation of the Saints, where we get refreshed and also shape future events.

A major highlight of this convention, is the building of our convictions, enabling us to contend for the faith delivered to us.

These 3 days of fellowship in the Spirit, is characterized by insight into Divine mysteries and demonstration of the Holy ghost and power.

In these 3 days as the lord has promised, there will be an harvest of foot soldiers, Souls and diverse miracles.

It is a must attend,come as we shape coming events through the word in an atmosphere of absolute possibility and sustain that which we have received of the lord.

Blessings
Pastor Dafe Richard


Sprit Life Convention

Spirit Life convention is an annual convocation of saints, for a renewing, refreshing and the strengthening of our convictions to enable us contend for the faith that was delivered to us.

These days of fellowship in the Spirit is characterized by insight into to Divine mysteries,a palpable presence of God, that does not leave anyone same way they came and most of all the outpouring of the holy spirit as captured in the old convenant Joel 2:28 and a first installment experince in the new convenant Acts 2.

In these three days as the lord has promised we will experince even more than what we have read in the scriptures.

It is a must attend, if we must shape the coming events and sustain that which we have recived of the lord.

Blessings
Pastor Dafe Richard
